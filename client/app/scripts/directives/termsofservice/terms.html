Iora Lite Terms and Details

About

Iora Lite is a powerful, web-based tool that lets anyone explore and tune the performance of dashboards and reports in a Salesforce org(anization). Iora Lite fetches a list of dashboards and reports in your org, analyzes and rates their performance, and advises you how to tune them so that they run faster.

Current version: Iora Lite 1.0
Developed by: Prakash Ramaswamy and Steve Bobrowski
Distributed under the Open Source BSD License

Data and Metadata

Iora Lite does not access nor does it store any data related to your Salesforce org. Iora Lite merely scans metadata for components in your org and provides you with assessments that subsequently vanish when you end your browser session.
